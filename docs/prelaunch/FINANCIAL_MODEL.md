## Financial Risk Factors

### 1. Market Risk
- **Risk**: AI operations market doesn't grow as projected
- **Mitigation**: Diversify use cases, build for general workflow automation

### 2. Competitive Risk
- **Risk**: Microsoft, Google, or Amazon launch competing product
- **Mitigation**: Focus on multi-cloud, open core, team-centric features

### 3. Churn Risk
- **Risk**: Higher than expected churn in Year 1
- **Mitigation**: Invest heavily in onboarding, customer success from Day 1

### 4. Pricing Risk
- **Risk**: Market expects lower pricing than modeled
- **Mitigation**: Freemium model allows price discovery, flexible enterprise deals

### 5. Technical Risk
- **Risk**: Infrastructure costs exceed projections
- **Mitigation**: Usage-based pricing passes costs to customers, optimize architecture

### 6. Funding Risk
- **Risk**: Unable to raise Series A at projected terms
- **Mitigation**: Achieve clear milestones before fundraising, maintain lean operations
