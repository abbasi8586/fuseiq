## Pricing Strategy

### Tier Structure

| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Agents | Executions | Users | Best For |
|------|--------------|--------------|--------|------------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | $0 | 1 | 100/mo | 1 | Individual exploration |
| **Starter** | $29 | $290 | 5 | 1,000/mo | 3 | Small teams |
| **Professional** | $99 | $990 | 25 | 10,000/mo | 10 | Growing agencies |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | ∞ | ∞ | ∞ | Large orgs |

### Pricing Psychology

- **Free tier** — Removes friction, allows viral sharing
- **Starter anchor** — Makes Professional look like great value
- **Professional as hero** — Most features, best value
- **Enterprise gate** — "Contact us" creates exclusivity

### Billing Model

- **Subscription** — Base monthly fee
- **Usage** — Overage at $0.01/execution beyond plan limit
- **Annual discount** — 2 months free (17% savings)
